Welcome to Born in Hawaii

We are a Native Hawaiian family-owned business, deeply rooted in our rich heritage. Our mission is to share the spirit of Aloha through our unique products and stories.

About Us

Born in Hawaii was established by our Ohana (family) to represent the people and culture of Hawai'i. Each family member has a role in our small business. Dad, Jeremie Makepa is the designer and Mom, Kahelelani Makepa runs the whole operation with the help of our 3 kids, Wailana, Waika, and Wahianu. Born Hawai'i designs are inspired by our beautiful place we call home, HAWAI'I.
